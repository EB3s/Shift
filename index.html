Common questions

Shift is an app that lets you book a trusted plumber, electrician, cleaner or nail tech in seconds — and watch them arrive in real time. No ring-arounds, no waiting in all day, no cash-in-hand gambles.

Every professional on Shift is vetted before they can take a job — identity, qualifications and work history are checked. You'll also see real ratings from other customers before you book.

You pay securely in the app at a clear, up-front price — no surprise charges. Your money is held safely and only released to the pro once you confirm the job's done right.

If a job isn't right, you raise it in the app and we step in — fast and fairly. Because payment is protected until you're happy, you're never left out of pocket.
